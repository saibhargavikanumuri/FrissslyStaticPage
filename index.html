About Us

We make your food habits healthier!

At Frissly, we are pioneering a revolution against packaged and chemical-laden foods. Our mission is to reshape dietary habits by making pure, organic, and wholesome food conveniently accessible to everyone. We offer a diverse range of products, celebrating the rich culinary traditions of India.

We are using ingredients cherished in ancient Indian cuisine, such as whole grains like Millets, Red Rice, Whole Wheat, Honey, Jaggery, Coconut Sugar, and Palm Sugar. We adhere to the highest standards of sustainability and transparency, ensuring that every bite of Frissly food not only nourishes the body but also nurtures our communities and the planet.

Our Process

24-hour Delivery Promise

We take pride in our meticulous process, ensuring that your food is as fresh and wholesome as possible. Our journey begins with sourcing the finest Organic and non-GMO ingredients. We manufacture our products in small batches within 16 to 24 hours of receiving your order, maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and quality.

Our eco-friendly packaging further ensures that the food you receive is not only good for you but also for the environment. Finally, our commitment to a 24-hour delivery promise guarantees that you get the freshest products delivered right to your doorstep.
